ABOUT ME

Hello, i'm Luke & I'm a junior games developer from the UK. I've always had a passion for games since I was a child, my passion has only grown since I entered university and started the games developing journey. I'm currently still at university ( University Of Central Uclan ) in my 3rd year then moving swiftly onto the masters programme.

Here you can see some of the projects I've been working on or projects I've had the opportunity to taken part in, this may be professional projects or personal projects this may also give some insight to the games I enjoy. I enjoy learning new things such as programming techniques, engines or different mechanics and taking on new challeneges. I like adding further polish to projects to get the desired outcome of the project and for it to be as good as it can be.
